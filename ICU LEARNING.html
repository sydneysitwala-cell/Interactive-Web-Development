Check for any water leaks on mechanical seal, pump casing, valves, reducers, stubs, rubber bellows, suction and discharge pipes. Any water leak off on these components shall indicate failure and corrective action shall be taken accordingly. Check for the quantity of water leak off on stuffing box for if pump uses gland packings and adjust the gland follower bolts if there is excessive leak off. In the case of pumps that use gland packings a predetermined amount of like off shall be allowed so as to lubricate the gland packings. Where there is excessive leak off gland follower bolts shall be tightened one turn at a time and the leak off shall be observed until the desired leak off is achieved.
